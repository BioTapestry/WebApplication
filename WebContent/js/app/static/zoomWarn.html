You've just used <Ctrl> {ZOOMTYPE} to zoom. Please note, this will only change the web browser's zoom settings (eg. font sizes and image display sizes), which can result in a 'low resolution' or blurry look for the model. <Ctrl> {ZOOMTYPE} will not zoom the network model itself.

To zoom in or out on the model, click anywhere on the model to focus it, and use the - (minus) or + (plus) key without pressing the <Ctrl> key.
